Here's what I specifically do

Frontend Web Development

I build custom websites based on HTML and CSS (Tailwind CSS) with little to no .Js for a quick and response touch. When Javascript is needed I reach out to Trent from Gezel.io for his expertise.

General Web Development

I spent most of my childhood building websites to insert iframes of my favourite games rather than going to each games website to play them. Dial-up was slow, and no one had time for MiniClip or Kongregate to load, so why not build a landing page with all these games. Load the page once, play for hours.

This lead me down a path of "no-code" web development, including my found love for WordPress on release.
Since this I've always leaned on WordPress for my main site building, un less something custom is required. But for blogs and other basic websites it's a CMS that I rely on very often.

Graphic Design

I personally use Affinity Design/Photo to design all logos and artwork on our websites (when not using FontAwesome, of course). I started out my designing in college working on local band logos and album art which led me down a road of eventually doing Twitch/YouTube creator art, including overlays and such to now a more business style icon designer. I'm never satisfied with a design, and will always be changing the artwork regularly, especially for Gezel.io.

Technical Support

I pride myself in my support skills. As someone who's always worked in the field, finding problems and resolving issues for users has always been a passion of mine. No matter how big or small the problem, once I sink my teeth into it, finding a resolution has such an affect of my mentallity I crave it everyday.
